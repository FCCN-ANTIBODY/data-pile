# Acceptance guidance — cd04-q1 (the mini-CONSTITUTION)

Prose companion to `guidance.json`. The machine reads the JSON; this explains intent. Editing
either is your right as the pile owner — the change lands in the git log and re-filters on the next
ingestion (`guidance_version` advances). You are master of what enters, not hostage to it.

- **Answers I accept** are one of the listed options (`a`, `b`, `c`). Anything else is
  `off-guidance`.
- **Scope.** Only responses tagged for `cd04` are in scope; others are dropped as `geo`.
- **Originality.** I keep only responses the respondent marks as their own original answer
  (`original: true`); the rest are dropped as `other`.
- **One per respondent.** Responses carry a `nonce`; the first wins, later duplicates (even via a
  different Tell) are dropped as `duplicate`.

What this guidance may ask for is itself bounded by [`CONSTITUTION.md`](../../CONSTITUTION.md) — and,
where this pile is matched through an Atlas, by that Atlas's CONSTITUTION. A pile cannot write
"accept everything" and harvest without a question and the respondent's consent.
